Unified list, sorted by urgency. Status transitions in real time as flights are detected, projected, and either confirmed or dropped:

  • 📅 planned watchlist entry expected within ~1 h; no live ADS-B match yet.
  • 📡 radio in ADS-B range, projected to pass within the panel band (≤ 2°) of the body — early-warning.
  • ✈️ candidate projected to pass within 0.3° (tight transit band); camera-prep.
  • 🎯 imminent closest approach within ±30 s; press the shutter.
  • ❌ stale contact lost — held while coasting through a brief gap, then ≤ 30 min before it drops.
